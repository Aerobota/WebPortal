      Buildings Energy Assessment

      The SUNSHINE platform will allow automatic large-scale assessment of building energy behaviour based on data available from public services.

      The information on energy performances will be used to automatically create urban-scale ecomaps to be used for planning activities and large-scale energy pre-certification purposes.

      Energy Consumption Optimisation

      The building energy assessment will be used, together with localised weather forecasts available through interoperable web-services, to ensure optimisation of energy consumption of heating/cooling systems.

      Through automatic alerts that will be sent to the SUNSHINE App the final users will be updated about the heating/cooling systems performances.

      Public Illumination Systems

      SUNSHINE will ensure interoperable control of public illumination systems based on Automatic MeterReading (AMR) facilities remotely accessible, via interoperable standards, from a web-based client as well as from an App for smartphones or tablets.

      This smart service will yield an optimisation of power consumption through remote control of public illumination levels.
